Thesis

During my undergraduate studies, I completed my thesis titled “A Notification Based Non-Restrictive Intervention Approach for Handling Excessive Smartphone Use.” The work explored how awareness-based notifications can help mitigate excessive smartphone usage without imposing restrictive controls. As part of the research, I conducted an online survey to understand user awareness of smartphone usage and developed an Android application called App Usage Tracker to monitor usage behavior. A three-week experimental study was designed using this application to collect real-world smartphone usage data and evaluate the effectiveness of the proposed non-restrictive intervention approach.

In addition to my thesis work, I have completed several academic and software projects. A collection of these projects can be found on my GitHub.
